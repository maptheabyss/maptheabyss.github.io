Hi there!

This is your basic blog highlighting recent advancements in ocean mapping, as well as historical achievements in oceanography. While I expect the number of people this blog will reach will be low, you can expect to update it regularly!

My blog is powered by jekyll and hosted on github. I know very very very little about both of these environments. So in a way, this blog entirely is a learning experiment for me -- I hope I acquire lots of knowledge! Along these lines, expect the layout and style and even content to change without notice as my expertise grows. Thanks already to Jonathon Mcglone for the great tutorial for getting this site up and running. Also to Tom Preston-Werner, creater of Jekyll (and a general blog inspiration). Last but not least, danke to Zach Holman. I'm expecting my thanks list to grow every week.
